Proudly Indigenous Nigerian Excellence

GGI PAINTS is a premium paint brand proudly manufactured by WALEGGI AND SONS INDUSTRY, an indigenous Nigerian manufacturing company dedicated to producing high-quality paints and allied products for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.

Founded on the principles of quality, integrity, and innovation, we are committed to delivering coating solutions that combine exceptional durability, superior coverage, vibrant colours, and lasting protection. Our products are manufactured using carefully selected raw materials and modern production processes to ensure consistent quality and customer satisfaction.

At GGI PAINTS, we understand that every surface deserves the perfect finish. That is why we continually invest in product development, quality assurance, and industry best practices to provide reliable solutions that meet the evolving needs of homeowners, contractors, architects, developers, painters, and distributors.

As a proudly indigenous manufacturer, we are passionate about contributing to Nigeria's industrial growth by creating value through quality products, skilled craftsmanship, and outstanding customer service. Our commitment extends beyond manufacturing to building lasting relationships with our customers, business partners, and communities.

Whether you are painting a home, office, commercial facility, or industrial project, GGI PAINTS is your trusted partner for premium-quality finishes that stand the test of time.
